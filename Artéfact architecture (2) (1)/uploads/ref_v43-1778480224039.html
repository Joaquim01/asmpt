Catalogue applicatif — Détail Cloud vs On-Premise par SCOR

As-Is Tool Fragmentation

No unified MES in Munich: Uptime, ELAM, Job Dispo, KANBAN, SAP Leitstand coexist across SCM sites
UK deviates most: WC/TCE, NCR, iDCR/iDOES, ProWorks, Cognos, Fixed bin — no Uptime, no Q-DB
Supply universally manual across all sites — highest automation potential
Q-Database widespread (Munich+SGP+MAL) but not consolidated — duplication with SAP

S/4HANA & AI Transformation Implications

MES Consolidation: Unify Uptime / ProWorks / ELAM onto one platform per site
BI Harmonisation: Modaly (Munich/SGP/MAL), Cognos (UK), Excel → SAP Analytics Cloud
Workflow Automation: replace manual Supply processes & PDF work instructions
UK Integration: highest deviation requires dedicated Change Management
